BLE MQTT Gateway

This is the MQTT BLE Gateway. The product uses the ESP32 SoC Chip and transfer the data of peripheral bluetooth device to cloud server. Also it sends the user command to bluetooth device. The product is manufactured by order in Zhuhai Factory.
